I was born and raised in Mount Angel, Oregon, a little town in the valley of the Pacific Northwest. I am a mother of four grown children and the wife of an amazing man. As a Military Spouse I have spent over 15 years trying to help Military Families be ready and resilient.

I started with SheCodes, wanting to change my life in a way that brings me joy and satisfaction. I encourage you to look through my portfolio. You will see my work from the very beginning stages and the progression of my skills as I continue to learn. I hope you enjoy!
